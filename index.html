Get Started

Resume and SoP Review services

We are offering profile-focused Resume and SoP reviews for students and early-career professionals applying to industry roles, placements, and higher-education programs (MS/PhD) in India and abroad!

Get Started

How it works

01

Submit

Use the form to submit the document you want us to review and tell us about yourself
Include as much information as you can about your background and projects in your resume or SoP drafts, including details you're unsure about. At this stage, completeness matters more than polish. Don't worry about length - we'll help you prioritise, refine, or remove content later.
Clearly state what your target programs, roles or companies are so we can tailor our offering accordingly
02

Review & Refine

We will review your document line by line and provide an annotated version with feedback via email (athaley.lagwankar.reviews@gmail.com) within 3-5 working days
Receive comprehensive feedback including content order and prioritisation, program- or role-specific tailoring, grammatical and clarity improvements and much more!
Additionally, we would also provide ATS-friendly resume templates in Latex and Word for the applicant to use
03

Revise & Resubmit

After reviewing our feedback, incorporate the changes you agree with and email us your updated document
We will review the revised version and help you refine it further, with up to three rounds of review included

Fill the form and get started on refining your journey!
All the submissions are processed on a first-come, first-served basis

About Us

Having gone through competitive universities, placements, and global applications ourselves, we understand how difficult it can be to get honest, experienced feedback on your applications. We created this service to offer aspiring engineering students and graduates the guidance we wish we had, helping them position their profiles clearly and confidently for their next step!
