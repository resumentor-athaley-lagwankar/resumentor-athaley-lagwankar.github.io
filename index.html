How it works

01

Submit

Use the form to submit the document you want us to review and tell us about yourself
Include as much information as you can about your background and projects in your resume or SoP drafts, including details you're unsure about. At this stage, completeness matters more than polish. Don't worry about length - we'll help you prioritise, refine, or remove content later.
Clearly state what your target programs, roles or companies are so we can tailor our offering accordingly
02

Review & Refine

We will review your document line by line and provide an annotated version with feedback via email (athaley.lagwankar.reviews@gmail.com) within 3-5 working days
Receive comprehensive feedback including content order and prioritisation, program- or role-specific tailoring, grammatical and clarity improvements and much more!
Additionally, we would also provide ATS-friendly resume templates in LaTeX and Word for the applicant to use
03

Revise & Resubmit

After reviewing our feedback, incorporate the changes you agree with and email us your updated document
We will review the revised version and help you refine it further, with up to three rounds of review included

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this service for?

This service is for engineering students and recent graduates applying to higher-education programs (MS/PhD) or early-career industry roles in India and abroad.

What documents do you review?

We review resumes/CVs and statements of purpose or motivation letters. You can submit either one document or both together.

How is this different from using AI tools?

AI tools help with wording. Our reviews focus on how your profile is interpreted by recruiters or admissions committees, including credibility, prioritisation, and role or program fit, based on real experience.

How long does a review take?

You will receive the first round of feedback within 3-5 working days from submission.

What does one “review round” mean?

One review round includes your submission/resubmission, followed by our review and annotated comments on the updated document.

How many review rounds are included?

Each submission includes up to three rounds of review, as long as the target role or program remains the same.

Will this guarantee an admit or job offer?

No. We help strengthen how your profile is presented, but final outcomes depend on multiple factors beyond our control.
